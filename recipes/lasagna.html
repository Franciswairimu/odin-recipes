Easy Homemade spiced Lasagna

Lasagna is a classical Italan dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy bachamel and melted cheese perfect for gatherings or a comforting personal or even family meal. This hearty casserrel offers a delightful blend of flavors and textures

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients

    2. You'll need lasagna noodles, tomato sauce, ground meat (typically beef or pork), ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, onion, garlic, salt, pepper, and optionally, herbs like basil or oregano.

  2. Prepare the meat sauce

    3. Brown the ground meat in a skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, and cook until they're soft and translucent. Pour in the tomato sauce and any desired seasonings, like salt, pepper, and herbs. Let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes.

  3. Prepare the cheese mixture

    4. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, eggs, salt, and pepper. Mix until well blended.

  4. Prep the noodles

    5. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they're al dente. Drain and lay them out flat on a baking sheet or parchment paper to prevent sticking.

  5. Assemble the lasagna

    6. Start by spreading a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Then, layer noodles over the sauce, followed by a layer of the cheese mixture and a layer of mozzarella cheese. Repeat this process until you've used up all your ingredients, finishing with a layer of sauce and mozzarella cheese on top.

  6. Bake

    7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in a preheated oven at around 375°F (190°C) for about 25-30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking for another 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

  7. Rest and serve

    8. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. This allows the layers to set and makes it easier to serve.
